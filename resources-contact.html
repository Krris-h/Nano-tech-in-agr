Short Notes

Ready-made Points

  • Nanofertilizers = nano-scale nutrient carriers (1–100 nm) with controlled release.
  • Main goal: increase efficiency, reduce environmental pollution and improve soil health.
  • Users: Farmers (primary), researchers, policymakers (secondary).
  • Expected outcome: higher crop yield, better quality, higher net returns.
  • Impact: less subsidy burden on government and ecological safety.

Viva Questions

  • Q: What is the size range of nanomaterials?
    Ans: 1–100 nanometres.
  • Q: Why are nano fertilizers called “magic bullets”?
    Ans: Because they can deliver nutrients directly to plant targets with high efficiency and low losses.
  • Q: Name two advantages of nano fertilizers.
    Ans: Higher nutrient-use efficiency and lower environmental pollution.
  • Q: Mention one risk related to nano fertilizers.
    Ans: Possible toxicity and accumulation of nanoparticles in soil and food chain.

FAQ

Are nano fertilizers safe for humans? +
Safety depends on product design and dose. Approved products are tested, but still, guidelines and protective measures follow karna zaroori hai.
Can small farmers also use nano fertilizers? +
Yes, lekin unhe sahi training, demo plots aur extension support milna chahiye. Dose and application method ko strictly follow karna important hai.
What crops are suitable for nano fertilizers? +
Cereals, pulses, oilseeds, fruits, vegetables – almost all crops, but exact recommendation crop and region ke hisaab se change hoti hai.
