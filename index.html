Nano Fertilizers – A Promise for Future

This portal is designed for farmers and students to understand how nano-fertilizers can increase productivity, protect soil health, and reduce the subsidy burden on government – all with ecological safety.

  • Department: Agriculture & Cooperation Department
  • Users: Farmers, Extension Workers, Agri Students
  • Focus: Efficient, safe, and smart use of nanofertilizers

Challenge Context

Why nano-fertilizers are needed?

Conventional fertilizers are often overused, leading to:

  • High chemical residues in soil and water
  • Health concerns for humans, animals and ecology
  • Low nutrient-use efficiency and wastage
  • Heavy subsidy burden on government

Nanofertilizers, also called magic bullets, provide high efficiency with low residues due to their large surface area and controlled release behaviour.

Expected Outcomes & Impact

Ye project farmers ko scientifically guide karega ki nano-fertilizers ka sahi istemal kaise karein – taki unki income bhi bade aur environment bhi safe rahe.

Expected Outcomes

  • Farmers can use nano-fertilizers more effectively for crop production.
  • Minimum chemical residue in soil and crops.
  • Improved soil health and long-term productivity.
  • Higher net returns due to better input-use efficiency.

Impact

  • Increase in farmers' income with optimized fertilizer usage.
  • Reduced fertilizer subsidy load on government.
  • Ecological safety and healthier environment.
  • Benefits for entire mankind through sustainable agriculture.
