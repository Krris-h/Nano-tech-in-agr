Step-by-step Algorithm for Using Nano Fertilizers

Step 1: Identify crop type and recommended fertilizer dose (kg/acre ya kg/ha) from govt / university recommendation.

Step 2: Check soil test report (agar available ho) – N, P, K aur micronutrient status dekhna.

Step 3: Decide nano fertilizer product (N, P, K, Zn, etc.) and its efficiency factor (e.g. 0.5 or 0.7 of normal dose).

Step 4: Calculate required nano fertilizer dose using formula (niche calculator me diya hai).

Step 5: Choose method of application: basal, top dressing, foliar spray or fertigation.

Step 6: Apply nano fertilizer as per product label – timing and dilution strictly follow karein.

Step 7: Observe crop response (colour, growth, yield components) and record data.

Step 8: Next season ke liye dose fine-tune karein based on recorded performance and advisory.

NOTE: Nano fertilizers ko hamesha certified products ki list se hi choose karna chahiye aur label instructions + local expert advice follow karna zaroori hai.

Normal vs Nano Fertilizer Calculator

Ye calculator sirf educational purpose ke liye hai. Field me apply karne se pehle official recommendation zaroor follow karein.

(0.5 = 50% of normal dose, 0.7 = 70% of normal dose)
Enter data and click Calculate to see the result.
Algorithm used in calculator:
1. Read area (A), normal dose per unit (D), efficiency factor (E).
2. Normal requirement = A × D.
3. Nano requirement = Normal requirement × E.
4. Saving = Normal requirement − Nano requirement.
5. Show all three values in table form.
